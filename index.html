🧱 Dance With Minecraft Blocks — How to Play

🎯 Goal:
Use hand gestures to shoot balls and topple the block walls.

🕹️ Controls:
👋 Wave Left Hand — Shoot a blue ball
👋 Wave Right Hand — Shoot a red ball
🦘 Raise Both Hands — Shake the walls

📸 Requirements:
- Webcam access
- Use body and hand gestures clearly in view
🟢 Green dots show tracking landmarks

🔐 No worries, just fun!
